Who is this and what does he do?

It is Yasin and I am living in Umeå/Sweden. I have 2 master’s degrees from Umeå University in the Human Geography department. The first one is specialized in Tourism while the second one is in Geographical Information Systems (GIS). I have a bachelor’s degree from Istanbul University’s Faculty of Economics Tourism Management program. I have also my high school degree from a vocational high school of hotel management and tourism which is the first of its kind in Turkey.

My work experience in the tourism industry exceeds 10 years. My last 2 jobs (bartender and flight attendant) cover 6 years in total and both full-time. During my high school and earlier university years, I have done internships at a hotel and an airline company office, and worked part-time at several MICE tourism and cruise ship travel agencies. In those years, I learned to work in groups effectively, took responsibility during stressful operations, set schedules, hired part-time staff, trained new team members and developed my skills in hostmanship. Consequently, I gathered experiences from diverse aspects of local and international tourism, and I would like to have the chance to utilize them in academic avenues.

Some of the qualities my studies equipped me includes applying advanced spatial analysis along with the knowledge of cartographic convention for a representation on a map form; post-processing by using Adobe Photoshop; collecting data in various forms and processing to use in GIS; building and modifying custom tools by using Model Builder; creating, processing, and analysing 3D data; and creating, modifying and analysing transport networks. I can also work with the Python programming language both in the GIS context and in general. Due to the context of the majority of my assignments at school, I am familiar with Swedish data sources and language. Besides the studies, I gave cartographic support to a PhD study (Safe Step Project) which had contacted our program coordinator for a freelance service from a student.

Currently, I am working on my programming skills for using GIS more precisely and effectively, and to be able to automize repetitive tasks in general. I am planning to learn working with APIs, big data and AI as well. I am also working on my language skills in Swedish.
